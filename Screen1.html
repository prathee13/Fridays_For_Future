ABOUT

Fridays For Future - Canada


In August 2018, Greta Thunberg began the "Fridays For Future" movement. Canada's first climate strike event in solidarity with Greta was Friday, November 2, 2018. By Friday, December 7, 2018, there were nine youth groups in Canada striking with Greta. Many youths in Canada are now striking regularly. Canada's national climate strike was Friday, May 3, 2019 and there were 98 events across Canada.
