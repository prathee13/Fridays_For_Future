Fridays For Future - Canada


In August 2018, Greta Thunberg began the "Fridays For Future" movement. Canada's first climate strike event in solidarity with Greta was Friday, November 2, 2018. By Friday, December 7, 2018, there were nine youth groups in Canada striking with Greta. Many youths in Canada are now striking regularly. Canada's national climate strike was Friday, May 3, 2019 and there were 98 events across Canada. Canadian youth are now preparing for Earth Strike anticipated on September 27, 2019.

DEMANDS


Follow Paris Agreement & IPCC Report

A landmark study by the UN that states we have 11 years to mend our ways.

Stay below 1.5 C

"If the earth goes above 1.5 degrees celsius change, we will have runaway global warming that will not be able to be stopped"

Focus on equity & justice

As stated by the Paris Agreement, no manifesto can ever be radical than equity or justice.
Unite behind science

Because science is beyond stupidity.
